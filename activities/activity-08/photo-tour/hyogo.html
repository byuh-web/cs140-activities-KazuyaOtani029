Himejijyou

Himejijyou is a historic castle in Himeji City, Hyogo Prefecture. It is one of Japan's most famous castles and is known for its elegant white appearance. It was built in the 14th century and has been preserved as a UNESCO World Heritage Site. One of the most famous castles in Japan. After sightseeing at Himeji Castle, you can explore the city of Kobe, so it's recommended.
