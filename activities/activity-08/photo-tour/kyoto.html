Kinnkakuji: Kinkaku-ji, officially known as Rokuon-ji, is a Zen Buddhist temple located in Kyoto. A Zen temple whose top two floors are completely covered in gold leaf, making it shine brilliantly in sunlight.

Kiyomizudera: Kiyomizu-dera is one of Kyoto’s most iconic and dramatic Buddhist temples—famous for its huge wooden stage and panoramic views. Kiyomizu-dera blends faith, engineering, and nature in a way that feels both powerful and peaceful—huge, yet somehow gentle.
