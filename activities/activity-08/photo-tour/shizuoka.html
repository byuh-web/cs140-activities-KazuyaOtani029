Mount Fuji

Mount Fuji (Fujisan) is Japan’s most famous natural landmark and a powerful national symbol. Mount Fuji represents beauty, endurance, and spiritual strength—simple in shape, huge in meaning. I climbed it on a camping trip when I was 15. My impression is neither good nor bad.
