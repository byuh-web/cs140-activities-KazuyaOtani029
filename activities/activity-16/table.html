History & Preparation

Japanese cuisine has developed over centuries through cultural exchange and tradition, focusing on simplicity and seasonal ingredients. Japanese Cuisine — Britannica

Dish Origin Main Ingredients How to Make
Ramen Originated from Chinese noodles and developed in Japan. Britannica Wheat noodles, broth, pork, green onions Cook noodles → prepare broth → add toppings
Sushi Originated as preserved fish food and evolved in Japan. Britannica Rice, raw fish, seaweed, soy sauce Season rice → shape → add fish → serve
Onigiri Traditional Japanese portable rice food used for centuries. Britannica Rice, salmon, umeboshi, seaweed Cook rice → shape → add filling → wrap
